§ utctime

typedef long shyft::core::utctime

utctime basic types for time handling we use linear time, i.e. time is just a number on the timeaxis, utc. Timeaxis zero is at 1970-01-01 00:00:00 (unix time). resolution is 1 second, integer. also define min max and no_utctime

The advantage with the definition is that it is well defined and commonly known in all platforms Also considered: moving to std::chrono, would enable a strict time arithmetic regime, of little advantage in this context, and at the cost of compile time (also consider the python/api part).
